Welcome to SB Central!

This website is meant to be your all-in one shop for your science bowl needs. If you are new to scibowl, check out this guide for new players and teams.

Some of the things you can do on this website is create a random packet out of our thousands of questions, play against friends, read textbooks, look at various stats, find upcoming tournaments, or find questions on our database.

Things to Check Out Here:

  • Create a random packet out of our thousands of questions
  • Play against friends
  • Read textbooks for science bowl
  • Look at various stats
  • Find upcoming tournaments
  • Use our native buzzer for online or in-person buzzing
  • Find and search questions on our database

You can sign up for an account here! If you already have an account, login here. To find out more about what an account can do, check out the about page.

If you are interested in helping with the website, submitting questions, announcing a tournament, or have any questions, please contact me on discord (@xpoes).
